## Description
My proposal is a procedural flower garden, where flowers are generated with random characteristics and grow over time. Each flower is created from a "seed" 
that stores its position, size, number of petals, color, and maximum growth. As the program runs, the flowers gradually increase in size until 
they reach full bloom, creating a dynamic and animated scene. The environment includes a simple sky and grass background, while each flower is uniquely generated,
making the garden visually varied every time the program is run. This project combines animation, randomness, and drawing techniques to simulate natural
growth in a stylized and interactive way.

## Features
- Procedural Flower Garden
	- Each flower is randomly generated with randomized traits such as petal count, color, and scale, creating a unique composition
    every time the program runs.

- Growth Animation System
	- Flowers simulate natural growth through gradual scaling over time, mimicking organic blooming and adding a sense of life and movement to the piece.

- Planting Mechanics
	- Users can interact with the canvas by placing flowers through mouse input, transforming the program into a participatory art experience.

- Custom Visual Enviornment
   - A stylized background with sky and grass establishes a cohesive visual setting, reinforcing the natural theme while maintaining a digital aesthetic.

- Visual Variation
  - The use of randomness ensures that no two gardens are the same, emphasizing unpredictability and generative creativity.


## Challenges
- Translating natural growth patterns into algorithmic behavior.
- Balancing randomness with intentional design for visually appealing results.
- Combining animation and interaction within Pygame’s rendering system.


## Outcomes
Ideal Outcome:
